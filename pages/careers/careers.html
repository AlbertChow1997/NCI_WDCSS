Mayor Square Guinness -- Job Opening!


Mayor Square Guinness is a high-quality restaurant&bar blending modern European culinary techniques with a warm, social atmosphere. We specialize in craft beers and creative Western cuisine. Located in the city center, our restaurant features rustic wooden décor, an open kitchen, and live music performances, offering an immersive dining experience. We value teamwork, employee growth, and customer satisfaction. Due to business expansion, we are now hiring passionate individuals to join our team!
