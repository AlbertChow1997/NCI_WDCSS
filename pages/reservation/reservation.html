Our main bar and restaurant offers casual seating and dining throughout the day offering teas and

coffees, sandwiches, small bites, traditional Irish food and of course a nice smooth pint of Guinness.

Friday & Saturday night. Walk-ins are welcome, however reservations are suggested to ensure your

table. If you're planning a group event with 10 people or more, please use our private event inquiry
