The Mayor Square Guinness

Welcome to the historic Mayor Square Guinness located on the banks of Mayor Square, Dublin 1. The Mayor Square Guinness has been extensively refurbished to incorporate a range of dining and function room areas, and to make the most of the panoramic views of the Dodder. We have lots of High Definition LCD TV screens so come and join us to cheer on your favourite team, horse or master golfer in great comfort with incomparable atmosphere!

History

The Mayor Square Guinness is located in the heart of Dublin's historic docklands area. The building was originally constructed in the early 20th century as a warehouse for the Guinness Brewery. In the 1990s, the building was converted into a pub and restaurant, and has since become a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.
